AVCOE

Amrutvahini College of Engineering (AVCOE) in Sangamner, Maharashtra, established in 1983, is a well-regarded autonomous engineering institute permanently affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University. Known for quality education, AVCOE offers various B.E. and P.G. programs, holds NAAC 'A+' accreditation, multiple NBA accreditations, and focuses on holistic development with strong industry ties, comprehensive placements, value-added courses like foreign languages (Japanese/German), and excellent infrastructure including state-of-the-art labs and hostels, aiming to groom technically proficient engineers.
